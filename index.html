Frequently Asked Questions

What's your turnaround time?

Our standard turnaround time is upto 24 hours when there is no urgency. For urgent requests we can deliver within 6 hours depending on complexity.

What will be the format of the logo?

We can deliver in any format you require (EMB, DST, PES, EXP, PNG, JPG, PDF, AI, etc.). Tell us your machine/model and desired format and we'll provide the correct file.

What payment method do you use?

Contact us via email for payment details. We provide invoices digitally before payment.

What's 3D (puff) embroidery vs regular embroidery?

3D or puff embroidery uses foam under the stitches to create a raised, three-dimensional effect ‚Äî commonly used on caps. Regular embroidery is flatter and suitable for most garments.

How can I place the order?

Send your artwork and instructions to unidigitizing2525@gmail.com. Include size, placement, formats, and turnaround preference.

What digitizing software do you use?

We use industry-standard tools such as Wilcom to ensure machine-ready files.

What file formats are supported?

Common formats include .png, .jpg, .cdr, .pdf, .ai, and machine formats like .dst, .pes, .emb.

How can I convey instructions to the designer?

Please include any of the following when you send artwork:

  • Design size: cm, inches, or mm
  • Placement: cap, left chest, full back, beanie, etc.
  • Garment type: towel, blanket, t-shirt, pique knit, fleece, etc.
  • Desired output file(s): .emb, .pof, .dst, .pdf, etc.
  • Turnaround time: standard (24 hrs) or rush (12 hrs or less)
